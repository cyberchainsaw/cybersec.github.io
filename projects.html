Homelab

My homelab consists of 6 Dell Optiplexes clustered together and running proxmox as an operating system. These machines are on the IPFire LAN. I am running both as a router and firewall appliance. I use this environment to build up and break down machines as needed for offensive and defensive practice. One of these involves a cluster of machines I use for forensic work. I have 2 machines, one running Flare, a Windows-based forensic image that includes many pre-built tools for malware analysis and reverse engineering. The other being REMnux, or the reverse engineering distro for Linux, being the Linux flavor for Flare.
